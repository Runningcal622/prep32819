Cairo Techniques

  1. For displaying exponential growths in an easily readible manner you can use a Logarithmic scale. This reduces exponential growth into linear growth. People see lines much better than exponentials.
  2. Another way to help visualize the data is to use indexes. By visualizing the indecies it allows the viewer to understand a series of datas. They can use the indecies to understand how the data is trending.
  3. Sometimes line graphs include a lot of volatility, making them hard to understand and see trends. One way to make this better is to use a trend line that follows the behavior of the graph, but uses a running average to smooth out the jags. Without the random peaks and valleys the relationship and stoty you are trying to tell is made easier.
